  • Portable Crushing Plant Flow Chart For Philippines

Portable crushing plant because it does not need the infrastructure construction, can directly open to the mining site, effectively reduce the transportation cost, strong adaptability to various environmental advantages, by the majority of customers pursued in Philippines. It is a set of production units with high efficiency, low cost, set feeding, crushing, screening, conveying in one. According to the demand of production it can be equipped with jaw crusher, impact crusher, cone crusher and so on, can be fast and other models matching combinations, meet different production requirements.

Portable crusher plant flow chart for Philippines as follows: firstly, mobile crushing with vibrating feeder materials are uniformly, continuously fed into the crusher; after primary crushing material with the vibration sieve processing, particle size to meet the requirements of the belt conveyer to material stack, as the final product, grain size of unqualified returns to the jaw crusher once again the broken. This constitutes a closed-circuit crushing, screening system, to realize the circulation of the material crushing, improve raw material utilization and product uniformity. According to the actual production of different needs, can remove the vibration sieve, change into other equipment. Primary crushing material passes through a jaw crusher, crushing equipment directly with other specific operation. The biggest advantage of our products is that it can be well combined with other equipment, consisting of different production line, convenient, flexible.
