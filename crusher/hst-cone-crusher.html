HST Cone Crusher

Type: cone crusher

Model Number: HST

Feed size: 10-560mm

Production Capacity: 30-1000t/h

HST cone crusher, short for single cylinder hydraulic cone crusher is the latest cone crushing equipment. The series cone crusher combines American technology, be the layer crushing an important breakthrough of principle and static pressure theory. Crusher cavity according to the crushing size requirement is divided into four cavity type hydraulic cone crusher, crushing, respectively, in fine crushing, extra fine. HST cone crusher is suitable for crushing Platts hardness of f=5-16 such as iron ore, non-ferrous metal ore, granite, limestone, quartz rock, Sha Yan, pebbles and other kinds of ore and rock, widely used in metal mines, metallurgical industry, chemical industry, building industry, cement industry, sand industry etc.

HST Cone Crusher Technical advantage

Big Crushing Ratio And High Production Efficiency

The HST cone crusher cavity shape machine adopts composite design unique, than the old spring cone crusher with high yield of 35%~60%, and the best product grain shape. This crusher combining speed and stroke higher, greatly improving the rated power, crushing machine through the production capacity and crushing ratio.

Low Cost Of Repair Wear Parts

All parts are hard to wear of the cone crusher, the repair cost is reduced to a minimum, general service life can be increased by more than 30%.

Adapt To A Wide Range Of Product Size

Design of four kinds of crushing cavity, crushing cavity can be obtained from the standard super thick cavity type to the short head superfine cavity arbitrary transform, to adapt to the large range of product size requirements.

Convenient Replacement

HST cone crusher all parts are available from the top or side crushing cone disassembly and maintenance, convenient disassembly and assembly, no need of dismounting machine frame, a fastening bolt, so the hydraulic cone crusher liner daily replacement more convenient.

Using Hydraulic System

Hydraulic system is used to make the hydraulic cavity cleaning and hydraulic protection. A high degree of automation, hydraulic control the discharging port of a wide range of simple and convenient adjustment. The overload protection of the crusher operation level has been greatly improved, so that the repair is more simple, more convenient operation, less machine downtime.
