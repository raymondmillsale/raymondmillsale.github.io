K Portable Crusher

Type: portable crusher

Model Number: K series

Feed size: 10-930mm

Production Capacity: 50-650t/h

K series tire mobile crushing station is the mainstream equipment in portable crusher industry. Its host can choose jaw crusher, impact crusher, cone crusher or vertical shaft impact crusher, material through feeder uniform transported to crusher be broken later with the circular vibrating screen to form a closed-circuit system machine, realize the circulation of the material crushing, meet the size requirements of the material by the output of belt conveyor.

Applications

  • K portable crusher is widely used in mining, coal, garbage and construction waste recycling, earthwork, urban infrastructure, roads or construction sites and other site work.
  • The portable crushing plant treatment of topsoil and a variety of other materials; separation viscous aggregate coagulation; construction and demolition industry; screening after crushing; quarrying industries.
  • Portable crusher is used for river pebbles, rocks (limestone, granite, basalt, diabase, andesite, etc.), ore tailings, the artificial sand stone chips.

What's The Features Of Portable Crusher

Portable crusher set by the material, crushing, conveying and other process equipment as a whole, by optimizing the process of making it has excellent rock crushing, crushing performance aggregate production, open pit mining, and different models can be combined to form a powerful crushing lines, to meet the widest range of coarse, medium and fine crushing and screening requirements for materials, multiple integrated automated materials handling, saves about 90 days of the preparatory period for customers, but also saves the installation, debugging and other steel based, high cost, equipped with independent power generation unit, convenient operation, convenient repair, quality stable and reliable.
