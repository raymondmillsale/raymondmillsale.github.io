The quartz sand processing equipments is quartz sand making machine. Breaking, shaping the series of sand making machine is suitable for soft or hard and super hard materials. It has the advantages of simple structure, high efficiency and energy saving, high efficiency, large output; and has finely, coarse grinding function, can complete the different particle size of sand stone production; the adaptability is strong, through the broken material ability, moisture of material by the impact of small, water content of up to 8%; but in crushing hard, very hard material (such as corundum sintered bauxite, etc.).

Quartz sand making production line process is: stone by crushing machine for a broken, produced into a coarse material by belt conveyor to the finely machine for further broken, after the stone into the vibrating fine screen gives two stones. Meet the sand gravel sand mechanism of feed particle size of sand making machine, do not meet the feed particle size of sand making machine material material returning again fine broken. Sand stones are made a part of sand, the sand washing machine (optional) is made after finished cleaning sand, the other part of unqualified products sand broken again, until the specifications meet the requirements so far.

The quartz sand production line a high degree of automation, low operation cost, high crushing rate, simple maintenance. The combination of quartz sand sand making machinery and equipment of flexible, can be combined according to the specific requirements of customers. In the operation process of the equipment, the customer must pay attention to whether the normal operation conditions of equipment. If abnormal, should be timely stop check. Also pay attention to problem of machine maintenance and maintenance etc. Good maintenance can greatly enhance the performance and the service life of the machine.
