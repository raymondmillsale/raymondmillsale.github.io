Coal processing has two forms, here descripe flow chart of coal processing plant:

Industrial process is the coal after the coal crusher, with the belt conveyor to quantitative feeder, to pressure the ball machine feeding by quantitative feeder uniform, sometimes need to improve the coal briquette strength, therefore, quantitative feeder and intermediate pressure ball machine can also add adhesive pot, even give into the adhesive, from the pressure ball machine out the finished product is the final product.

The people with the industry machining process and industrial coal by principle of coal basically is the same, the difference is the raw coal after crusher, need of raw coal and loess a uniform mixing, stirring after can enter the briquette, briquette machine out from product to the final product.

Both in industrial application are very extensive, larger number. But with the improvement of rural living conditions and the level of economic increase, and the development of clean energy, coal briquette machine used in civil industry use became subject to certain constraints, but in our country, the honeycomb briquette is still one of the main forms of folk with coal.
