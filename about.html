Welcome to SBM

Shanghai SBM Mining and Construction Machinery Co., Ltd is a hi-tech, engineering group. We are specialized in the research, development, and production of industrial crushing, grinding mill, mineral processing equipments and other related devices. Our products have been sold to 120 countries.


Our Technology

New products development is a key part of SBM culture since it was founded. SBM R&D team is made of 78 senior engineers. The top seven engineers are winners of special central government allowance, which are for the outstanding contribution in science and technology.

Recent development includes the K Portable Crusher, PE Jaw Crusher, LM Vertical Grinding Mills, etc. we are pursuing the "SBM direction of technology and quality" all the way.
