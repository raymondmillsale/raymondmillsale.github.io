HJ Jaw Crusher

Type: jaw crusher

Model Number: HJ

Feed size: 150-800mm

Production Capacity: 110-650t/h

With reliable performance, reasonable design, convenient operation, high efficiency characteristic of crusher. In the system of sand production line, HJ jaw crusher is used for primary crushing large stone. Stone by vibrating feeder evenly sent to the series jaw crusher for coarse broken, broken after the coarse material by belt conveyor to the HJ jaw crusher cavity second for further broken, after fine crushing material is transported to the vibrating screen screening, to finished product size requirements of the material is fed into the sand washing machine cleaning, finished product size required materials from sieve return sand making machine re processing does not reach, the formation of many closed loop, finished product size can be combined and classified according to the needs of users.

HJ Jaw Crusher In Sand Production Line Advantages

The series jaw crusher discharge particle size is smaller, while feeding a larger particle size, low mining costs, HJ jaw crusher in sand production line has a long history , the advantages of this device is high yield, feed size large, crushing the material evenly, easy maintenance and so on, especially for large rocks, ore crushing.
